Who am I?

Hi there! My name is David Serrano, born in 1990, and I am a person who does the things he loves with great passion.

My greatest objective is to develop the best thing yet. Either be it a video game, a web-site, a television set interface, the best web service.

In the Internet I am known as the N0_Named_Guy. I am a computer science student, taking a masters at the moment. I've graduated from FCT-UNL and I hold a bachelor at computer science.

Residential Website

 (August 2010)

Based of the Auto Guinot website, I made a site for a residential called Nosso Paraíso, located in the sunny Algarve. During my stay, I proposed the residential owners to build them a site. I took the Auto Guinot's code base, and over the week I stayed there, I made them the website. It is (or at least was) hosted at www.residencialnossoparaiso.com.

Auto Stand Website

(from June 2008 to May 2009)

A website I made on my free time for an auto stand. It was originally hosted at www.autoguinot.pt, but the stand owners stopped caring for the website, so the website is down at the moment.
